An official website of the State of California

The .ca.gov means it’s official.
State of California government websites always use a .ca.gov domain. Before sharing sensitive information online, make sure you’re on a .ca.gov site by inspecting your browser’s address (or “location”) bar.

This site is also protected by an SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) certificate that’s been signed by the government. The https:// means all transmitted data is encrypted — in other words, any information or browsing history that you provide is transmitted securely.
